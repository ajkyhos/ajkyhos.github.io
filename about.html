A Personal Story

My name is Alex Kyhos and I grew up in a small town in the center of Wisconsin. Both of my parents own and operate a local hardware store which acted as both a first job and a second home. I was very active in high school with clubs and organizations, leading to lots of opportunity outside the town. Since I also loved going to Badger Football games, UW Madison had always been one of my top school choices after graduating. Following graduation, I decided to persue a degree in Communication Arts at UW Madison which has opened many doors for potential career paths. My time at Madison has been nothing short of extrodinary, as I remain involved in as much as I possibly can. I am an active member of the UW Marching Band, a talent booker and videographer for WUD Music, a graphic designer for the College of Engineering, a sound designer for Badgermation, and an active member of the Hollywood Badgers.
